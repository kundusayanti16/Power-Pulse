Empowering Consumers

We are dedicated to building a smarter, more transparent, and highly responsive electricity grid for everyone. Your voice helps us keep the lights on.

What is the Smart Electricity Portal?

The Smart Electricity Consumer Complaint & Feedback Portal is a centralized digital platform designed to bridge the gap between electricity consumers and utility providers. It replaces slow, traditional phone-based complaint systems with an instant, geo-located digital reporting tool. Whether it's a neighborhood power outage, a faulty meter, or dangerous voltage fluctuations, this platform ensures your issue reaches the right engineers immediately.

🎯

Our Mission

To eliminate blackout wait times by providing utility companies with real-time, actionable data directly from consumers, ensuring faster dispatch and resolution.

🛡️

Transparency First

No more guessing when your power will be back. Every complaint receives a unique Tracking ID, allowing you to monitor its status from "Pending" to "Resolved" in real-time.

⚙️

Smart Technology

We utilize intelligent routing and automatic geolocation to pinpoint the exact source of outages. Our system automatically escalates unresolved complaints after 72 hours.

How It Serves You

  • Geo-Located Reporting: Automatically captures your coordinates so engineers know exactly where the fault is located, saving hours of manual searching.
  • 24/7 Accessibility: File a complaint or log feedback at any time, from any device. No need to wait on hold with customer service.
  • Automated Escalation: If a filed complaint is not resolved within the standard service window, the system automatically highlights it for senior administration.
  • Live Grid Statistics: View public dashboards showing how many issues are currently active in the grid and our overall resolution rate.

Types of Issues Handled

  • Complete Power Outages: Report total loss of electricity in your home or entire neighborhood.
  • Voltage Fluctuations: Report dimming lights or power surges that could potentially damage sensitive appliances.
  • Transformer Sparks/Fires: Urgent reporting for dangerous hardware failures on street poles.
  • Faulty Meters: Request maintenance for meters that are stopped, broken, or displaying incorrect readings.
